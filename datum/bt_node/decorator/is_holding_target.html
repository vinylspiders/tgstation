is_holding_target
/datum/bt_node/decorator/is_holding_target

Gates child on the pawn currently holding the item at the given blackboard key in its hands. Clears the key and returns BT_FAILURE if the item has been deleted. Returns BT_FAILURE without clearing the key if the item exists but is not held the item may be in a bag or on the floor and other branches may still need to locate it.
